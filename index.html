about us

Bun Bun Bake Shop was founded in 2015 by a bunch of graduate students at Carnegie Mellon University. What was once a fantasy between a group of sleep-deprived friends turned into a thriving business. We wanted to give back to the Pittsburgh community by creating warm, comfortable space to relax with friends, not to mention our award-winning pastries!
